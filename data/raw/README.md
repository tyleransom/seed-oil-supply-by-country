"Pre" data (1961 - 2013) is accessed from https://www.fao.org/faostat/en/#data/FBSH.

- under "countries" select "Select All"
- under "elements" select "Food supply quantity (kg/capita/yr)" and "Food supply (kcal/capita/day)"
- under "items aggregated" select "Grand Total + (Total)"
- under "items" select:
    * Soyabean oil
    * Groundnut oil
    * Sunflowerseed oil
    * Rape and Mustard oil
    * Cottonseed oil
    * Sesameseed oil
    * Ricebran oil
    * Maize germ oil
    * Oilcrops oil, Other
- under "years" select "Select All"
- click "Download Data"


"Post" data (2010 - ) is accessed from https://www.fao.org/faostat/en/#data/FBS following an identical process
